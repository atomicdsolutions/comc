Walktober

Miles for the Mind

Our signature step challenge and wellness walk takes place every October, rallying Black and Brown communities, caregivers, advocates, and supporters to move for mental health and break down stigma—one step at a time.

How It Works:

1

Participants join individually or create teams

2

Track steps and movement throughout October using the StepUp app

3

Collective goal: Walk thousands of miles together and raise funds

4

Top teams and individuals win prizes, recognition, and swag

Caregivers Deserve Care

This year's Walktober includes a special "Miles for the Mind" 5K for caregiving awareness, honoring those who support others while highlighting their own mental health needs.

Goal: Beat 30,000 Miles!

Silent Auction

Giving Tuesday Edition

Our Annual Silent Auction is the heart of our Giving Tuesday campaign, bringing the community together to support mental wellness for Black and Brown communities through 100% virtual participation.

How It Works:

100% virtual event—participate from anywhere

Bid on items from Black and Brown-owned businesses

Every bid funds mental health programs and outreach

Build economic empowerment and community partnership

Why It Matters

Our biggest fundraiser of the year, timed to amplify Giving Tuesday—a global day of giving.

By featuring products from businesses that share our mission, we build economic empowerment and community partnership alongside mental wellness.

Removing the Stigma

Open Conversations for Change

In-person forums and panel discussions with licensed mental health professionals that create safe spaces for open dialogue about mental wellness. We normalize these conversations and provide practical resources for our community members.

Monthly forums with licensed therapists and counselors

Panel discussions on culturally competent mental health

Q&A sessions with community mental health experts

Resource sharing and referral networking
