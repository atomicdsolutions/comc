About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a licensed clinical social worker with over 8 years of experience providing trauma-informed therapy to Black communities. She specializes in helping clients navigate racial trauma, cultural identity challenges, and intergenerational healing.

Dr. Johnson believes in the power of culturally responsive therapy that honors clients' backgrounds while providing evidence-based treatment. Her approach integrates traditional therapeutic methods with cultural healing practices.
