Meet Dr. Rodriguez

Dr. Maria Rodriguez is a licensed psychologist with a passion for strengthening families and relationships within Brown communities. With over 10 years of experience, she brings both clinical expertise and deep cultural understanding to her practice.

As a first-generation immigrant herself, Dr. Rodriguez understands the unique challenges faced by families navigating multiple cultures, languages, and generational differences. Her bilingual practice serves families who want therapy that honors their heritage while addressing modern relationship challenges.

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in family systems therapy, helping families communicate across cultural and generational lines while maintaining their cultural values and strengthening their bonds.
