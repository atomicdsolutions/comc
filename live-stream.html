Community Guidelines

Creating safe spaces for everyone in our live streams

We Welcome:

Respectful questions and comments

Sharing personal experiences (when comfortable)

Supportive responses to others

Cultural perspectives and insights

Please Avoid:

Harmful or discriminatory language

Providing medical advice or diagnoses

Spam or promotional content

Triggering content without warnings
