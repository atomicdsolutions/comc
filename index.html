The Challenge We Face

Mental health stigma in Black and Brown communities creates real barriers to healing

60%
of Black adults avoid mental health treatment due to stigma
3x
less likely to receive culturally competent care
75%
report feeling misunderstood by providers

Our founders experienced this firsthand

Two people searching for community and struggling to find therapists who truly understood their cultural background. That search became a podcast, and that podcast became a movement.
