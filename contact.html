Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For crisis support, our text line is available 24/7 at 555-HELP.

Do you provide direct therapy services?

We connect people with culturally competent mental health providers and offer community support programs. We maintain a directory of diverse therapists and can help with referrals.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers for various programs including community outreach, event support, and advocacy efforts. Contact us at volunteer@gmcbomc.org to learn about current opportunities.

Do you accept donations and are they tax-deductible?

Yes, we accept donations to support our mental health advocacy work. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all donations are tax-deductible. Contact donations@gmcbomc.org for more information.

Ready to Get Started?

Whether you need support, want to volunteer, or are interested in partnering with us, we're here to help you make a difference in mental health advocacy.
